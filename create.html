Your Essay.

This is not a bio. It is a portrait of your mind. Take your time—quality is the only filter we have.

Writing Guidelines

  • Avoid physical descriptions. Focus on how you think and feel.
  • Write at least 300 words. Depth requires space.
  • Be honest about your flaws. Vulnerability is the shortcut to trust.
  • Discuss your curiosities, your obsessions, and your quietest joys.
