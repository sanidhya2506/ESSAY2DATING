How it works

1

Write your story

No photos allowed. Submit an essay about who you are, what you value, and what makes you tick. Your words are your only currency here.

2

Get a Vouch

Invite a friend to submit a "Character Reference" for you. It adds a layer of trust and lets others see you through the eyes of someone who loves you.

3

Connect with Depth

Browse the Reading Room. If an essay resonates with you, request a connection. If they agree, we introduce you via email. You choose when to swap photos.

Why no photos?

On apps, we judge people in 0.5 seconds based on lighting and angles. That's not dating; that's shopping. By removing photos, we force a focus on personality, humor, and intellect.

We believe that a great conversation is the most attractive thing in the world. This is for those who agree.

Common Questions
